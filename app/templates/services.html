Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI emotion detection?

Our AI emotion detection algorithms have been trained on extensive datasets and refined through clinical validation. They achieve an average accuracy rate of 85-90% in detecting primary emotional states. However, we always emphasize that AI analysis should complement, not replace, professional mental health assessment.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, we take your privacy extremely seriously. All data is encrypted end-to-end, and we adhere to HIPAA-compliant protocols. Your personal information is never sold to third parties, and you have complete control over your data, including the right to delete it at any time.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll immediately gain access to all premium features. When downgrading, you'll maintain access to premium features until the end of your current billing cycle.

How often should I use the services?

For optimal results, we recommend daily mood tracking and weekly in-depth assessments. However, the frequency can be adjusted based on your personal needs and schedule. Our system will provide personalized recommendations based on your usage patterns and wellness indicators.
